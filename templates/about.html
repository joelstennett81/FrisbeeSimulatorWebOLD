About Us

Welcome to the Frisbee Simulator! This platform allows you to create, manage, and simulate frisbee games.

Creating Players

You can create players by clicking on the "Create Player" button. You'll be able to enter details such as the player's name, position, and other attributes.

Creating Teams

Once you have created players, you can create teams. Select up to 7 players for each line (offense, defense, bench) and assign them to your team.

Creating Games

With your teams ready, you can now create games. Choose two teams to play against each other and start the simulation.

Enjoy simulating your favorite frisbee games!
